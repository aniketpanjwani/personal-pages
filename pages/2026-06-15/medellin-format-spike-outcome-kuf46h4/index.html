Format Spike Decision Memo

Decision date: 2026-06-15
Decision owner: operator
Status: recommended for operator approval

Recommendation

Edition #1 should ship as a compact weekly briefing, not an event newsletter with extras. The locked v1 order should be: one-time reintroduction note, This Week's Signal, The Shortlist, Move Smarter, and Personal Note. This Week's Signal, The Shortlist, Move Smarter, and Personal Note are persistent launch blocks. Builder Profiles, reader-driven quests, housing/real estate intelligence, and AI-generated local-moment Reels are occasional or experimental lanes for Editions #2-#4 and should not block Edition #1.

Why This Shape

The six approved Taste Anchors argue for a newsletter that carries judgment, not volume. Medellingles and Winnipeg Digest both support a standing public-source-backed briefing block that translates local coverage into English-language practical meaning. Catskill Crew shows that a local newsletter becomes more memorable when it has a named lead, a recurring community mechanic, and a curated happenings layer rather than a flat calendar. Remote Roberto and Medellin Tour Guidee show that foreigner-facing Medellin content works when it names a specific anxiety and gives a trusted next move. Naptown Scoop shows how social can turn one local moment into a high-reach visual artifact, but its lesson is mainly for Instagram and later social production, not for changing the core email shape.

Operator taste overrides a pure traction reading in two places. First, the Edition should not chase Remote Roberto’s concierge/VIP/dating framing even though it clearly attracts attention. Second, the Edition should not become a Medellingles-style utility archive even though that model is directly adjacent. Medellin Insider’s sharper lane is a trusted English-language operator read on what is worth doing, what changed, and how to move through the city well.

Challenged Assumptions

  • Five events: Reframe to 3-5 curated Shortlist entries. Five remains allowed when there are five genuinely strong picks, but the default quality bar should be “worth leaving the apartment for,” not “fill five slots.” The full event database belongs in the app and later web surface.
  • One Nugget: Keep persistent, but rename and sharpen as Move Smarter. The job is one practical Medellin answer: a newcomer mistake, payment/timing workaround, safety pattern, housing note, neighborhood tradeoff, or local etiquette point.
  • One Personal Note: Keep persistent, short, signed, and email-exclusive. It is the relationship block and should not appear on Instagram.
  • Email-exclusive value: The email keeps the operator read, source interpretation, selection criteria, “why this matters,” and personal note. Instagram can tease the fact pattern or shortlist; app/web stores durable references and the broader event inventory.

Surface Decisions

  • Email: Holds the complete Edition: This Week’s Signal, Shortlist, Move Smarter, and the signed Personal Note. Email gets the operator read, context, criteria, and the reason a pick matters now.
  • Instagram: Uses unsigned Brand Voice. Best launch social derivatives are Shortlist carousels, This Week’s Signal explainers, and Local Moment visual posts. It should not publish the Personal Note or the full reasoning behind the Edition.
  • App / web: Holds the broader event database, saved events, evergreen guides, neighborhood and venue pages, source-linked practical explainers, and eventually reader-nominated guide outputs. The Edition should route readers to durable app/web references rather than trying to contain everything.

Deferred Ideas

  • Full Format Radar automation across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, newsletters, Reddit, and local media.
  • A standing Builder Profile block before there is a lightweight sourcing and outreach loop.
  • Standing real estate/housing coverage before disclosure, source-quality, and conflict-of-interest rules are clear.
  • A large local-newsletter benchmark database as a launch dependency.
  • Facebook-group extraction as a regular source. It is too painful relative to TikTok, Instagram, Reddit, WhatsApp, and deterministic public sources.
  • Automatic publication or event promotion from private-channel evidence.

Decision Status

This memo satisfies the launch-blocking Format Spike decision requirement once the operator accepts or edits it. Implementation work that depends on Edition composition should build against the block order above. Work that is agnostic to composition, such as subscribe-page, deliverability, event candidate pool, and renderer plumbing, can continue in parallel.

Revised Edition Requirements

Date: 2026-06-15 Status: proposed launch handoff Source: Format Spike decision memo and six approved Taste Anchor profiles

Purpose

This document updates the Medellin Insider launch Edition shape after the Format Spike. It is a product/editorial handoff for implementation and weekly drafting. It does not create new Convex tables, new public endpoints, or a new admin surface.

Locked Edition #1 Shape

Edition #1 should contain these blocks in this order:

  1. One-time reintroduction: a short “what this is now” note for the revived list, with a clear unsubscribe path.
  2. This Week’s Signal: one public-source-backed Medellin update or outside coverage translation with a clear audience implication.
  3. The Shortlist: 3-5 curated events or activities, each with a reason to care and a practical logistics cue.
  4. Move Smarter: one practical living-in-Medellin answer, internally still compatible with the Nugget concept.
  5. Personal Note: 2-4 signed sentences from Aniket, email-exclusive.

For Edition #2 and later, the one-time reintroduction disappears. The remaining four blocks persist unless the operator intentionally runs an experiment.

Block Requirements

This Week’s Signal

  • Must be based on public evidence or an operator-approved public-enough source.
  • Must answer “what changed or what matters this week?”
  • Must translate raw local coverage into practical English-language meaning for wealthy English-speaking nomads, visitors, English-speaking Colombians, founders, operators, investors, and globally minded locals.
  • May cover local news, business/culture changes, city services, transit, safety context, regulation, restaurant/venue movement, or a Colombia/Medellin article that needs local interpretation.
  • Must not be a generic headline feed.

The Shortlist

  • Default count is 3-5, not exactly five.
  • Every entry must be hand-approved and should clear the “worth leaving the apartment for” bar.
  • Every entry should include the practical reader fit: neighborhood, date/time sensitivity, price/ticket friction where known, and who should care.
  • The app and future web surface carry the larger event database; the Edition carries only judgment.
  • If fewer than three strong picks exist, the operator should either add a hand-written third item or intentionally ship a thin Selection with a visible editorial reason. Do not pad with weak events.

Move Smarter

  • Reader-facing label for the practical Nugget lane.
  • One specific answer per Edition: newcomer mistake, cost/payment workaround, housing note, safety pattern, neighborhood tradeoff, local etiquette, transport/timing move, or bureaucracy/visa/tax note.
  • Should come from the Nugget backlog, public research, WhatsApp/Reddit signal summaries, deterministic scraper context, or operator judgment.
  • Must be sanity-checked by the operator because practical advice creates correctness risk.

Personal Note

  • Must be written or explicitly approved by Aniket.
  • Should be short, first-person, and signed or clearly attributable.
  • Must not appear on Instagram.
  • Should invite replies without pitching consulting services.

Surface Routing

Email

Email is the complete Edition. It owns:

  • the signed Personal Note;
  • the full “why this matters” interpretation;
  • the selection criteria and practical caveats;
  • the most useful source links and context;
  • the explicit reply invitation.

Instagram

Instagram uses unsigned Brand Voice and should publish derivatives, not the entire Edition:

  • Shortlist carousel;
  • This Week’s Signal carousel;
  • Local Moment Reel/carousel;
  • Move Smarter teaser;
  • subscribe CTA.

Instagram must not include the Personal Note or the full email-only reasoning.

App / Web

App and future web surfaces own:

  • the broader event database;
  • saved events and favorites;
  • durable guides and practical explainers;
  • venue/neighborhood pages;
  • public source references;
  • future reader-nominated guide outputs.

Edition #2-#4 Experiments

  • Edition #2: turn one Shortlist or Signal into a saveable Instagram carousel.
  • Edition #3: test a Local Moment visual post from real stills or short clips, with generated motion only if it looks natural and does not restart a manual-production loop.
  • Edition #4: test one participatory prompt: reader-nominated guide, micro-quest, or Builder Profile candidate request.

Experiments should stop or change if they increase operator time, feel generic, attract low-quality promotion, or flatten Medellin into tourist scenery.

Not Launch Blocking

  • Standing Builder Profiles.
  • Standing real estate/housing coverage.
  • Full Format Radar automation.
  • Comprehensive local-newsletter benchmarking.
  • Regular Facebook-group extraction.
  • Automatic publication from private-channel evidence.

Implementation Notes

  • Keep the Edition as a git-versioned artifact.
  • Preserve operator approval before publication.
  • Keep private Research Signals and Evidence Pointers out of public outputs.
  • Keep Canonical Events separate from Research Signals.
  • Do not introduce new root Convex surfaces just to represent this decision.
  • Let the renderer/template work consume this block order, but keep the exact visual treatment flexible until the Social Content Factory review.

Launch Gate

The Format Spike launch gate is lifted when the operator accepts this revised shape or edits it into a preferred final form. The recommended next product task is to make the Edition file/template/rendering path support this block order without increasing weekly operator time.
